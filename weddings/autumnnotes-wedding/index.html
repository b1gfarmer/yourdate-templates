FAQs

Should we bring a gift?

You joining us to celebrate is gift enough and we mean that! But if you would still like to give us a gift, a contribution towards our honeymoon would be lovely.¬†

Is the ceremony and reception in the same place?

Yes! Our venue is hosting us for the whole day so once you arrive you won't need to worry about travel for the rest of the day.

Is there somewhere to leave our car?

Yes, there is plenty of free parking at the venue and cars can be left overnight even if you're staying elsewhere. We just ask you collect your car by 10am the following day.¬†

Can we stay at The Mill Barns?

There are only a limited number of rooms at the venue so if we haven't given you room booking details separately then, you can find recommended alternative accommodation below. ¬†

Are the kids invited?

We have invited little people by name on our invites and we'd love to have them party with us. But if Mum and Dad fancy a night off to let their hair down that's cool too!¬†

Can I bring a plus one?

We hope you can understand that we can only accommodate guests named on the invitations. ¬†

Can we post pictures on social media?

Sure! we can't wait to see all your snaps! Don't forget to tag us. We just ask that you do not take pictures during the ceremony until you're invited to do so.¬†

Will there be food catering to my diet?

Yes! We have delicious food options for all diets, please let us know any requirements when you RSVP. we will make sure you're well fed.

Can I get a drink at the bar?

Yes! The bar will be open for pre ceremony drinks from 12pm and all throughout the day! Please be aware The Mill Barns operate a card payment only bar

What if you haven't answered my question?

When you RSVP there is a space for you to leave a note for us, so if you have any questions we haven't answered you can drop a line there or give us a call or text.¬†
