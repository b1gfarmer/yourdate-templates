In the Andes foothills between the coffee-rich heartland of Armenia (Santi's family hometown) and Bogotá's crisp mountain peaks (Camila's hometown) lies our favorite city, Medellin. The city, where eternal spring paints the landscape with endless blooms, is a magical blend of old and new. It's a place bursting with life, boundless creativity, lush greenery and some of the world’s warmest and happiest people — our own kind of sunshine in human form. 
Your presence means the world to us. 
This celebration is also about you – the people we love. You've shaped our journey and enriched our story. We look forward to celebrating our love with you in our enchanted Bosque Bedoya. We hope you enjoy discovering the heart of Colombia through our eyes.

With (so much) love,
Santi & Cami
