Location

Walkabout Creek Discovery Centre is located 12km from Brisbane’s city centre at 60 Mount Nebo Road, Enoggera Reservoir.
Follow Waterworks Road from the city as it turns into Mt Nebo Road, not far from the entrance to the Walkabout Creek Discovery Centre.
Plenty of parking spaces have been reserved for our wedding guests.
